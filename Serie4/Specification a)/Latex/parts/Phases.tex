\section{Phases}

\newcommand{\phase}[4]{
\subsection{Phase #1}
\subsubsection*{Input}#2
\subsubsection*{Task}#3
\ifx&#4&%
\else
\subsubsection*{Output}#4
\fi
}



All Phases get one Parameter, the Parameter 
will have to provide Inforamtion based on the input section
this will correspond to a list of implemented interfaces
or requirements on the form of the Input.

Examples:

Graph $\to$ parameter implements IGraphInfo

\phase{Init}				{Graph}
{Load Options}
{Options}

\phase{CycleBreak}			{Graph}					
{Break Cycles by revercing edges.}
{Acyclic}

\phase{LayerAssignement}	{Acyclic, Graph}				
{Assigne Nodes to Layers}
{Layered}

\phase{UndoCycleBreak}		{Layered, Acyclic, Graph}		
{Load Options}
{invalidats Acyclic}

\phase{DummyNodePlacement}	{Layered, Graph}				
{Break Edges spanning multiple Layers into multiple smaller edges by inserting dummy Nodes}
{Dummy}

\phase{CrossingMinimization}{Layered, Graph}		
{Minimize Crossing of edges by swaping the order of nodes in a layer.}
{Ordered}
